What is this?

Revaluate is the easiest way of keeping track of your expenses without becoming a full control freak. You'll be able to track your expenses on the level that is good for you, not going crazy over every cent.

  • Effortlessly track your expenses.

    Track your expenses by adding the price, the category you spent that on and optionally a description that is more meaningful to you.

  • Watch the impact of your choices

    Get simple, yet meaningful insights regarding your expenses, to identify the categories you want to improve on.

  • Let us help you with your goals

    Define and then follow up with your financial goals in the easiest way possible.

  • Plan ahead

    You'll be able to plan your expenses ahead so you'll know what to expect each month.

  • Track missing money

    Track how you stand with your debts and the money you lent.

  • Share with others

    You're not living alone, so you'll be able to collaborate with your family or friends on your wallet.
